About This Property

Fully remodeled and impeccably designed, this Studio City residence blends modern style with everyday comfort just moments from Ventura Boulevard and convenient access to the 101. The open-concept layout showcases wide-plank wood floors, recessed lighting, and a showstopping chef’s kitchen with a quartz island, custom cabinetry, elegant hardware, stainless steel appliances, skylight, and picture windows that bring in abundant natural light. The connected living, dining, and family areas create an easy, functional flow, anchored by a white brick fireplace and expansive windows. The primary bedroom features direct backyard access and a spa-inspired bath with dual vanity, custom wood cabinetry, designer tile, and an oversized shower. Additional bedrooms and a beautifully renovated secondary bath provide flexible space for guests, workspace, or creative use. A private brick courtyard with custom pavers and water-wise landscaping adds charm and usability, while a long gated driveway leads to a detached two-car garage with ample parking and storage. Ideally located near popular dining, shopping, parks, and hiking trails, this home offers refined living in a desirable Studio City neighborhood.
