About This Property

Fully renovated and move-in ready, this single-level San Carlos ranch home offers the kind of space that makes everyday living feel easier, brighter, and more connected to the outdoors. Set on a large 10,000 sq. ft. lot, in a premier neighborhood, this home features 4 bedrooms, 2 baths and approximately 1,824 sq. ft., a fresh modern remodel, expansive front yard, and standout backyard. Inside, the home feels light, warm, and welcoming, with bright updated interiors and a comfortable open layout that brings the main living spaces together. The remodeled kitchen is both beautiful and practical, featuring new appliances, a gas range, white quartz countertops, gold fixtures, bar seating, and an easy transition to the dining area. The four-bedroom layout offers flexibility for everyday living, work-from-home needs, guest space, hobbies, fitness, or room to spread out. One of the home’s most inviting spaces is the light-filled sunroom. Opening directly to the backyard, it draws in views of the expansive yard, mature fruit trees, and private outdoor setting while creating a natural flow between the home and the yard. There's two separate grassy areas, mature fruit trees, privacy, and plenty of room for entertaining, outdoor dining, pets, gardening, play, or simply relaxing. Recent improvements include new flooring, remodeled kitchen and baths, new HVAC with AC, new electrical, and new windows. Located in San Carlos, close to shopping, dining, schools, parks, Lake Murray, Cowles Mountain, Mission Trails, and everyday amenities.
