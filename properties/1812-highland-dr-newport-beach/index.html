About This Property

Tucked into one of Dover Shores' most coveted streets, this Newport Beach residence is undergoing a complete designer renovation — reimagined with a design sensibility built around warmth, texture, and quiet refinement. The design centers on a kitchen with a large center island and dining banquette connecting two distinct living spaces — a family room with a fireplace and built-in shelving, and a vaulted living room anchored by its own fireplace. The primary suite, two additional bedrooms, and 2.5 baths are all on the main level. The primary is being finished as a genuine retreat — a spa-inspired bathroom and an oversized walk-in closet anchor the space. Each secondary bath is being designed with its own distinct finish language.A second-floor flex space adds versatility to the layout. Situated within Mariners Elementary's attendance boundary and minutes from the Upper Newport Bay Nature Preserve, the 17th Street corridor, and the Newport waterfront.
