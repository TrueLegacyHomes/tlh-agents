About This Property

A private 25' boat slip and 50 yards to Humboldt Beach — this thoughtfully remodeled home within gated Tennis Estates in Huntington Harbour offers rare harbor access paired with refined coastal living. Light wide-plank flooring runs through the open main level, where a fireplace with a sage green surround anchors the living room before flowing into a dining room framed by a shiplap wall and built-in dry bar. A breakfast room off the kitchen opens through sliding glass doors to a private patio. The galley kitchen is the design centerpiece: floor-to-ceiling white oak slim shaker cabinetry, white marble-look countertops, a glossy ceramic backsplash, and brushed gold hardware. Upstairs, the primary suite pairs a vaulted ceiling and wall of windows complemented by a dual white oak vanity ensuite, brushed gold fixtures, and a frameless glass shower with a built-in bench. The secondary bath offers its own distinct character with bold gray and white checkerboard tub surround, horizontal shiplap, and a rattan pendant, while a gingham-wallpapered powder room adds a stylish touch near the entry. An upstairs loft provides flexible additional living space. A tankless water heater and HOA-covered water, trash, dock maintenance, and access to a community pool, spa, tennis, pickleball, and basketball courts complete the picture. Located near Huntington Harbour Mall, Trader Joe's, and the beaches, this home delivers the lifestyle Huntington Harbour buyers rarely find available.
