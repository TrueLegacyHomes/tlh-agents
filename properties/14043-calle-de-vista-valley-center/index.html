About This Property

Set on 3.56 acres in the hills above Valley Center, this fully reimagined single-story ranch commands sweeping panoramic views of Palomar Mountain and the valley floor — visible from the front, the rear, and even the detached garage. From the covered porch, the home opens into a dramatic great room where vaulted, open-beam ceilings rise above light oak-toned plank flooring. Oversized picture windows frame the mountain panorama alongside a floor-to-ceiling stone fireplace. Flowing into the dining area, the fully redesigned kitchen brings together white shaker cabinetry, a white oak-toned island with bar seating, dark quartz countertops, counter-to-ceiling white ceramic tile backsplash, new stainless steel appliances, and polished chrome hardware — refined without being overdone. Down the private hall, the primary suite offers a walk-in closet and an en suite with dual-sink warm wood vanity, dark quartz countertops, and a walk-in glass shower clad in taupe-and-cream checkered tile. Two additional bedrooms share a full hall bath with tub featuring designer finishes. Out back, the rear patio opens to panoramic views with an outdoor kitchen, built-in grill, and sink. Up front, a second covered patio extends the living space toward the valley below. Attached garage includes a full bath and laundry hookups. Across the lot, the detached 3-car structure — outfitted with solar, a covered patio, half bath, and its own septic system — arrives ready for an ADU conversion or dream workshop, with acreage to spare for a future pool, RV storage, and whatever comes next.
