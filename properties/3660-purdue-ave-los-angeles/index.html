About This Property

From the two-tone charcoal and stacked stone facade to the designer-curated interiors, this Mar Vista home is where bold design meets everyday livability. Wide-plank engineered hardwood flows throughout an open layout where a custom quartz fireplace surround anchors the living room, and a dark board-and-batten accent wall with brushed gold sconces adds a moody, editorial counterpoint. The kitchen is a full-length galley that opens to the backyard through black-framed bifold doors, making it as much about the experience as the cooking. Custom white oak inset cabinetry lines both walls, with quartzite slab countertops that carry up as the range backsplash and zellige tile wrapping the refrigerator wall for a warm textural contrast. A 6-burner professional range with pot filler anchors the cooking side, while the island offers a natural gathering spot that ties the space together. The primary suite feels like a true retreat — bifold doors dissolve the boundary between bedroom and backyard patio, while the en-suite makes a statement with a floating white oak dual vanity, arched mirrors, and a frameless glass shower wrapped floor-to-ceiling in vertically stacked aubergine ceramic tile with a built-in bench. Each additional bath has its own personality: the hall bath pairs castle moss green ceramic tile with terrazzo-style porcelain floors and brushed gold fixtures, while the powder room goes full drama — deep burgundy walls, a sculptural cream pedestal sink, and black-and-white mosaic marble floors. Outside, flagstone pavers and fresh sod create an enclosed backyard made for California evenings, complemented by a finished detached two-car garage. New roof, HVAC, tankless water heater, and windows round out a renovation built to last. Moments from Mar Vista Recreation Center, the beloved Mar Vista Farmers Market, Culver City dining, and Penmar Golf Course — with Venice and Santa Monica beaches just a few miles away.
